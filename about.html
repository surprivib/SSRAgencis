About Company

We Sri Satya Rajeswari Group started both Pharma and Vaccines distribution in Visakhapatnam with strong & Experienced team.. Sri Satya Rajeswari  have exclusively started all types of  Pharma and Vaccines distribution with coverage of Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram and Srikakulam Districts of Andhra Pradesh. We are covering around 1000+ retailers which includes  Hospitals, institutions, Paediatricians, Gynaecologists and General Medicine and other specialities in Visakhapatnam Vizianagaram and Srikakulam Districts. All of our customers are having satisfying partnerships with us in terms of our Pharma supply, cold chain management and pricing.We have dedicated & committed staff around 45 employees in our Organisation.For Quick Deliveries we have our own transportation vehicles like Vaccine Refer Van,Auto & Motorcycles.
